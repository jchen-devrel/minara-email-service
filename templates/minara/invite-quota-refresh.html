Invite quota refresh is available

Hey there,

Good news — invite quota refresh is now enabled for your plan.

What this means: When you use up all your invites, your invite quota will automatically refresh back to your plan's quota. If you still have invites left, just keep using them as usual.

Quick reminder: Your invite quota is tied to your subscription plan. Higher plans get more invites.

Rewards at a glance:

  • On activation: you and your friend each get 200 Credits
  • On first subscription: you get 500 Credits + 50% of their Sparks bonus

Head to Profile → Invites to view your current invites and get your invite link.

Questions? See the Referral & Rewards docs or reach out on Discord.
