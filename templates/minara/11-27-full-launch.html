After months of building and testing with early users, Minara is now officially open to everyone. No invite codes, no waitlist — just Minara at minara.ai.

Thank you for helping shape this product. Your feedback, your ideas, and your daily use have played a huge role in bringing Minara to this moment.

Digital finance is fragmented across chains, protocols, and tools; most decisions are still stitched together from social feeds, research dashboards, and trading screens. Minara makes that a single, coherent flow: you describe what you want to do, and it uses 50+ data sources and finance-native, multi-agent reasoning to turn intent into analysis, strategy, and execution.

What you can do:

  • Ask and execute: Go from "what's happening with this token?" straight to placing trades in one natural-language conversation
  • Prompt-to-agent: Turn a single instruction into automated monitoring, alerts, or copy-trading strategies
  • Discover and track: AI-curated token picks, market signals, and cross-chain position monitoring across a wide range of digital finance assets
  • Deep research: Run multi-source, multi-turn research on tokens, wallets, and strategies, with structured summaries before you act

We're shipping weekly. AI Trading Copilot for perpetual futures is next — structured strategies and real-time intelligence when markets move fast ⚡
