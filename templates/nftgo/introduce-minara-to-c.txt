Minara, NFTGo, and DMind are sister projects under the same group, each serving a different layer of the ecosystem:

- NFTGo — market intelligence and analytics for NFTs and digital assets
- Minara — an all-in-one financial AI assistant spanning analysis → reasoning → execution
- DMind — open-source, finance-native models and data powering AI products in digital finance
What is Minara?
 Minara brings together everything you care about — crypto, stocks, DeFi, RWAs, NFTs, commodities, and more. She keeps you ahead of the market with real-time insights and helps you catch trends before they go mainstream. Beyond insights, Minara lets you trade seamlessly across chains and even build financial agents in plain words. She connects the full cycle — from analysis, to decision, to execution — all in one place.
What you can do with Minara
- Analyze with insight: Ask complex questions about tokens, wallets, or DeFi strategies and get insightful answers built from multiple data sources — so you see not just the what, but also the why.
- Stay ahead of the alpha: Track assets and markets in real time, set smart alerts, and follow wallets or collections to catch trends before the crowd.
- Trade smoothly: Place spot and limit orders, or copy-trade top wallets gaslessly across multiple chains, with an experience as seamless as your favorite exchange.
- Turn words into agents: Create simple agents in words to monitor wallets, trigger alerts, or automate strategies for you.
