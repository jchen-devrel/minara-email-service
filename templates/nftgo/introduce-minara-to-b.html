Hello NFTGo Partner,

You've known us as NFTGo, the leading NFT analytics and trading platform. NFTGo remains active and fully supported — we continue to maintain the datasets, dashboards, and APIs you rely on.

Over the past year, we've expanded beyond analytics into AI for digital finance. This effort led to Minara — an all-in-one financial AI assistant that combines deep analytics, workflows, and execution, powered by DMind, our open-source AGI research initiative building crypto-native models, benchmarks, and datasets for digital finance.

Minara, NFTGo, and DMind are sister projects under the same group, each serving a different layer of the ecosystem:

  • NFTGo — market intelligence and analytics for NFTs and digital assets (nftgo.io)
  • Minara — an all-in-one financial AI assistant spanning analysis → reasoning → execution (minara.ai)
  • DMind — open-source, finance-native models and data powering AI products in digital finance (dmind.ai)

What Minara means to you

  • Deeper analysis via APIs: unify 70+ curated data sources (on-chain flows, CEX/DEX markets, DeFi, sentiment, macro) with finance-native language models.
  • Workflow automation: convert complex monitoring/reporting into repeatable agents that collect data, reason, and alert.
  • Execution built in: run wallet-based strategies or place spot and limit orders across major chains with workflow automation through a secure custodial wallet framework.
